# Executive Summary

The internal security audit of Botium Toys identified significant gaps in the organization’s security controls and compliance posture.

The company currently lacks critical safeguards such as encryption, least privilege access, and intrusion detection systems, exposing it to high risk (8/10).

Immediate action is required to implement essential controls and align with regulatory standards such as PCI DSS and GDPR.

Failure to address these issues may result in financial loss, legal penalties, and reputational damage.
